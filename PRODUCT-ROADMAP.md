# Vidra Product Roadmap (Value-Driven)

## Positioning

Vidra should feel like a "creator game engine": users build a character, run campaigns, and optimize growth loops.

## FREE Forever (offline-only, Python-only)

1. Persona DNA presets with style archetypes and consistency scoring.
2. Rule-based monthly calendar generation with narrative arcs.
3. Caption mutation engine with tone sliders (confident, playful, cinematic).
4. Content streak system: daily score and consistency milestones.
5. "Creator quests": guided weekly objectives with measurable outputs.
6. Markdown/JSON exports optimized for manual execution in image tools.

## PRO (conversion-grade outputs)

1. LLM caption enhancement and hook optimization.
2. Trend-aware content suggestions from search/news signals.
3. A/B caption variants with confidence score.
4. Brand-safe mode with style and compliance checks.
5. Automated monthly strategy brief with growth opportunities.

## MAX (agency/scale mode)

1. Multi-persona portfolio board with calendar conflict detection.
2. Team workflows: role-based review queues and approvals.
3. Campaign simulator: expected engagement by content mix.
4. White-label exports and client-ready reporting bundles.
5. Autonomous scheduling assistant across personas and markets.

## Growth Mechanics To Add Early

1. Onboarding goal selection (followers, sales, authority) to personalize output.
2. Weekly "wins" report to increase retention.
3. Upgrade triggers based on pain moments (speed, quality, scale).
4. Referral loop: share public persona profile snapshot.

## Monetization Guardrails

1. Keep FREE genuinely useful with full offline generation for one persona.
2. Reserve speed, optimization intelligence, and scale for PRO/MAX.
3. Build transparent cost telemetry for external API usage.
